About Me

A Photographer
by Instinct

I'm Jane Doe, a documentary and portrait photographer based in New York. I've spent the last decade chasing light — from quiet morning streets to bustling markets across three continents.

My work explores the space between the expected and the unseen: the gesture right before a laugh, the stillness after a storm. I believe every photograph is a negotiation between the world and the moment you choose to freeze it.

Available for commissions, editorial work, and exhibitions worldwide.
