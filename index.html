Zoe Yang

Bay Area-based photographer driven by a childhood passion for capturing moments. This passion evolved into a profound interest in photography, especially focusing on urban architecture and cyber cities.

My background in interdisciplinary photography from Cornell University combines art, science, and engineering to create a unique style emphasizing symmetry and balance. My work aims to transport viewers into a serene aesthetic realm by abstracting subjects to amplify their essence.

Outside of photography, I enjoy fashion, tech gadgets, and golfing as an adrenaline enthusiast who embraces life on the edge.

Based San Francisco Bay Area, CA
Education Cornell University — Interdisciplinary Photography
Speciality Urban Architecture · Cyber Cities · Symmetry
